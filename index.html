BOOKING
Do you provide props?
Yes! For every event we curate a unique prop selection from our VERY large collection. I guarantee we have something for everyone & every event. All of our props are high quality & built to last the entirety of your party. No more feathers & sequins all over the dance floor at the end of the night. We even offer custom props if you need them.

Don’t like the idea of props & just want your guests faces (& outfits) to be the focus? Just let us know & we won’t bring any.
We want printed photos. Do you offer that?
Yes, our photo booth service is super customizable for your needs. While every event has a digital photo gallery, you can also add prints to any of our hourly packages. We offer both the classic 2x6 inch photo strips & standard 4x6 inch options.
